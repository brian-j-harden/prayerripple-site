Child Safety & CSAM Prevention Policy

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

PrayerRipple is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and take immediate action on any content or behavior that threatens the safety of minors.

Our Commitment

  • PrayerRipple does not allow the posting, sharing, or distribution of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form.
  • We actively moderate user-generated content to detect and remove harmful or illegal material.
  • We comply with all applicable laws and reporting obligations related to CSAM.

Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate or harmful content directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly by our team.

If we become aware of CSAM, we immediately:

  • Remove the content
  • Disable the user account
  • Report the incident to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Report to relevant regional or national authorities, as required

User Responsibilities

  • Do not upload or share any images, videos, or text involving minors in sexual or exploitative contexts.
  • Report any suspicious content or behavior using the “Report” feature inside the app.
