Account Deletion Request

You may request deletion of your PrayerRipple account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a request and what information is removed or retained.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account, send an email to:

support@prayerripple.com

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your PrayerRipple account
  • A brief statement requesting account deletion

Once your request is received, your account will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile
  • Prayer requests you have posted
  • Praise reports
  • Uploaded images and media
  • Friend connections
  • Notification history and activity logs

What Data May Be Retained

For legal or security reasons, some minimal data may be retained:

  • Billing or transaction records (if applicable)
  • Security and server logs (retained up to 30 days)

No retained data is used for any purpose after deletion.

After Your Account Is Deleted

Account deletion is permanent. If you return to PrayerRipple in the future, you will need to create a new account.
